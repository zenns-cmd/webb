All About Computer-Aided Design

What are the benefits of computer-aided design?

  • Allows experts to create more accurate design representations
  • Replaces manual design drafting, allowing design development, alteration and optimization.
  • Enables engineers to craft more precise designs and manipulate them virtually.
  • CAD software calculates how multiple materials relate.
  • CAD software is frequently used by different types of engineers and designers.
  • CAD software can be used to create two-dimensional (2-D) drawings or three-dimensional (3-D) models.
  • Time and cost efficiency
  • Improved machining capabilities.
  • Improved client accessibility.
  • Lastly, reducing material wastage.

Who uses CAD?

  • Civil engineers use CAD to see their sketch in 3 dimensions
  • Electrical engineers use CAD to see the kind of electronics they'll be working on

How to use CAD?

  • Firstly, find the right app for you and draw your sketch
  • Then, you can print your work on a 3D printer
